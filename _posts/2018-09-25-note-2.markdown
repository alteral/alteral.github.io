## Conclusion

The script has written in *Ruby* and claims a good mood. Putting it on our *CI* and running about once an hour, we can greatly reduce the load on the batteries of our test devices. Via it we request all the connected *Android* devices and get the current percentage of the charge. If it is less than *42* â€” we turn on the charge, if it is greater than *42* â€” we turn off the charge.
